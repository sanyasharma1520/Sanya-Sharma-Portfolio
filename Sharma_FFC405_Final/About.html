MULTIMEDIA ARTIST
Sanya Sharma is a fashion communications student, pursuing a Bachelors in Design at Toronto Metropolitan University. Through her background in the fine arts, in addition to her skills in graphic, product, and print design, she has been able to pursue incredible opportunities within the design field.

In the past year, she has spent her time involved in her school's creative collective. Some examples are her experiences participating in various exhibitions to display her paintings and jewellery designs. This is in addition to her position in a student-run fashion magazine as a Senior Graphic Designer, and as a curatorial, gallery assistant for The Creative School, in which she helps to organize exhibitions and create print and digital advertising materials.
